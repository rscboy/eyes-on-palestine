The Genocide Archive

In July 2026, the Israel Exposed collective released their war-crimes archive fully open source to resist censorship — cataloguing 94,000+ documented records of alleged war crimes across Gaza and the West Bank (2023–2026), drawn from field journalists and source channels. Echoes of Gaza cites and credits this archive as an independent, verifiable evidentiary collection. All footage and attribution belong to Israel Exposed and the journalists who captured it.

The curated set is an 864 GB signed media release (167,114 files), part of ~2.4 TB of raw source archives preserved by torrent and mirrored on Distributed Denial of Secrets. The website itself is now open source (MIT). Every release is cryptographically signed — verify authenticity before trusting any copy, and submit footage only to their official domains.
