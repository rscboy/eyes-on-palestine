What Is Echoes of Gaza?

Echoes of Gaza is a publicly accessible digital archive preserving Palestinian history in real time. We collect verified, time-stamped records—articles, testimonies, and cultural documentation—to ensure war crimes and lived experiences cannot be denied, rewritten, or forgotten.

Why This Exists
  • History is being erased and manipulated in real time
  • Evidence disappears via censorship and removals
  • Palestinian voices are routinely excluded
  • Accountability demands a permanent public record
This archive exists so the world can never say, “We didn’t know.”
The Difference
  • Verified Sources: Every entry links to evidence
  • Time-Stamped: A permanent chronological record
  • Open Source: Transparent and censorship-resistant
  • Living Archive: Updated daily by volunteers
  • Community Driven: Built for justice, not profit
How to Use It
  • Journalists & Researchers: Cite verified data
  • Educators: Teach from primary sources
  • Activists: Reference documentation
  • Community: Preserve stories
